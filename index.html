AutoPlay Express
Live Project

AutoPlay Express is a software tool to create simple and advanced autoplay menus for CD and DVD media. It will make your business card CDs, multimedia presentations or movies/pictures collections look and run professionally. The intuitive user-friendly interface allows to design autoplay menus fully visually and entirely in a point-and-click mode. Just type your texts and drop pictures, buttons and interactive/multimedia stuff to create autorun CD menu. With AutoPlay Express you see exactly what you get.

For more details visit kl-soft.github.io/autoplay-express
